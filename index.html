Time Remaining 10:00

“This Is Our God!”

10 Minute Discourse

10:00 LEFT

Introduction

Illustrate: Imagine a child being bothered by a bully, feeling completely helpless. But at the right moment, his father appears. That child is no longer disarmed; he is proud and confident because he knows who is standing behind him.

We have every reason to feel similarly about Jehovah. As you gain insight into his qualities, you realize you have the best Father imaginable. Our theme tonight is: "This Is Our God!"

Why does this matter? Because the more we draw close to him, the stronger our confidence gets. Tonight, we will look at what Jehovah gives us now and what he has in store for the future. This confidence motivates us to witness to his name at every opportunity. Notice how this joy is described in the Bible at Isaiah 25:9.

Read Isaiah 25:9

“In that day they will say: “Look! This is our God! We have hoped in him, And he will save us. This is Jehovah! We have hoped in him. Let us be joyful and rejoice in the salvation by him.””

Explain: We have every reason to be proud of our God and happy!

8:00 LEFT

1. Taste — Gift of a Creator

Lets talk about food! Who likes food? Why do you like food? Among all the senses, taste really shows Jehovah’s thoughtfulness and love. Jehovah knows that we don't just eat for fuel; he designed us to find joy in life. Notice how he describes his generosity at Isaiah 25:6.

Read Isaiah 25:6

“In this mountain+ Jehovah of armies will make for all the peoples A banquet of rich dishes, A banquet of fine wine,* Of rich dishes filled with marrow, Of fine, filtered wine.”

Explain: Taste not only protects us from harm but allows us to distinguish flavors like the sweetness of a fresh orange or the cool of mint. Each tiny taste bud contains about 100 cells triggering signals to the brain. This shows Jehovah wants us to enjoy an enriching dimension of life. In the new world, this sense will be fully satisfied. There will be an "abundance of grain" and "fine physical food" to enjoy under his Kingdom.

Illustrate: Imagine if eating was as boring as filling up our cars with gas. We would see we are getting low and then just fill up and walk away. Instead, eating is one of the great joys we have in life.

Apply (Ministry): During your daily routine, ask: "Do you ever wonder why we were made to enjoy food so much rather than just needing it for energy?"
5:00 LEFT

2. Swallowing Up the Shroud

We’ve talked about food, but we know that today, even the most joyful meal is eventually overshadowed by the passage of time. Jehovah promises to remove that shadow. Notice his promise at Isaiah 25:7, 8

Read Isaiah 25:7, 8

“In this mountain he will do away with* the shroud that is enveloping all the peoples... He will swallow up* death forever,+ And the Sovereign Lord Jehovah will wipe away the tears from all faces.”

Explain: sin and death are “the shroud.” But verse 8 says Jehovah will "swallow up death forever." He doesn't just push it back; he consumes it so it no longer exists. He "wipes the tears from all faces"—showing his warmth and empathy.

Illustrate: WE ARE COVERED IN A SHROUD. Our vision is skewed by sin. In paradise we will pursue anything: physics, chemistry, music, or art. Many say they wouldn’t want to live forever because they think it would be boring—their vision is skewed. We can share that vision with them!

Apply (Picture): Look closely at the older brother. Physically limited... medical needs... but notice: he has his Bible open. He is "bragging" to his caregiver about his Father. Even under the shroud of old age, his vision isn't skewed. He sees the "Grand Provider."
1:30 LEFT

Final Conclusion

How we delight in the many things Jehovah gives us! We are priviledged to be among the only group on the earth at the moment that worship him in the elevated way that he approves as prophisied at isaiah 2:2.

Just think about the overwhelming Joy we will feel when he resurects our loved ones and all the people in the world will be friends of peace. Thank Jehovah for our resurection hope!
